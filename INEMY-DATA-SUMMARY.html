💡 Key Insights

🏆 Largest Employer

Trade is the largest employer in Greece, representing 17.3% of total employment and employing over 700,000 people.

💰 Economic Powerhouse

Trade generates 37.1% of total economic turnover, contributing €179.5 billion to the economy in 2024.

👩 Gender Equality Leader

Retail trade promotes women's employment with 55.3% female participation, significantly above the 43.3% economy average.

📈 Strong Recovery

Trade showed strong recovery post-2020, with turnover growing 51.6% from 2020 to 2024, reaching new record highs.

🏢 Entrepreneurial Hub

27.3% of all employers in the economy operate in trade, making it a key driver of entrepreneurship and business creation.
